KIM JUNG SOOK GALLERY
JS GALLERY는 회화를 중심으로 작가와 작품, 공간을 하나의 흐름으로 잇는다. 작품을 나열하는 대신, 각 전시는 하나의 사유가 공간 안에서 작동하는 구조로 구성된다. 작가는 선택되는 것이 아니라, 전시 안의 관계 속에서 드러난다.
JS GALLERY connects artists, works, and space into a single flow centered on painting. Rather than arranging works, each exhibition is constructed as a structure where thought operates within space. Artists are not selected— they emerge through relationships within the exhibition.

Dungzak Cestlavie understands painting not as a simple image, but as a structure of thought. His work connects light, memory, and being into a single flow, where the surface becomes a field in which that structure appears.

The work is not an illustration for explanation, but a field where thought condenses; color, form, and rhythm emerge as the ways that thought operates. Within this structure, painting moves beyond the consumption of images and becomes a space that lets us read memory and being again.
